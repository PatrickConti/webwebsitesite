What is the Hobo Code?

Nestled amidst the rows of sky scrapers lining the Windy City, a crisp breeze flows into every pint we proudly serve at Hobo Code Brewing. One sip from our refreshing assortment of small batch brews will cleanse your palate and transport you to simpler times.


More than markings on a wall, The Hobo Code represents a part of our country's lost history. Never spoken, only lived, its glyphs guided many down the train tracks to a purer life. Like weary travelers from the past seeking respite, a warm meal, and a refreshing beverage, we invite you to come hop on at our Chicago Junction.
